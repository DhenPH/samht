ABOUT SERVER

Multi-World

Dive into multiple custom worlds, each featuring unique mob difficulty levels and rewarding loot drops! Battle through progressively challenging environments, defeat custom-designed enemies, and collect rare items in a dynamic Minecraft experience.


Semi-Anarchy

On Temporal Realms, PvP, griefing, and raiding are fully allowed — it's a true no-rules survival server where any player can attack or kill anyone at any time. Form alliances, betray friends, or survive solo in a chaotic open world where trust is rare and danger is everywhere.


Custom PVP

Battle in our custom PvP arena, where every victory earns you a prestigious Rank Tag! Climb the leaderboard, prove your skills, and gain recognition across the server. The more you win, the higher your PvP rank — show off your dominance in style!


Party System

Form a party with your friends, team up, and build your own massive base in a shared survival world! Whether you're defending against raids or creating an epic kingdom, collaboration is key. Work together to gather resources, design powerful bases, and dominate the server.


Warp/TPA

Tired of spending too much time walking or exploring the map manually? On Temporal Realms, you can use /TPA and warp commands to instantly travel across the world! Whether you're meeting up with friends or jumping to key locations, our teleport system saves time and keeps the adventure going.


SellGui/Auction

Our server features a SellGUI system that lets you easily sell loot and unwanted items with just a few clicks — no commands needed! Want to make big profits? List your rare items in the Auction House, where other players can bid and buy at premium prices. It's the perfect economy system for both casual players and seasoned traders!
